Privacy Policy


The Privacy Policy describes how Flipkart Internet Private Limited and its affiliates (collectively “Flipkart, we, our, us”) collect, use, share or otherwise process your personal information through the Flipkart website www.flipkartethics.com (Website). All ethics concerns/incidents reported to us will be treated with utmost confidentiality as possible, and if you choose, you may report your concern anonymously to the extent allowed by law.

Collection of your information

You can report an incident anonymously by writing to us at the email id and phone number provided in the Website, and choose to not provide any information that may uniquely identify you. However, when you chose to provide your personal information and use the Website to report an incident, we may collect, process and store the following categories of personal information that are legitimately necessary for us to fulfill our obligations:

  • Date / Time of incident
  • Personal Identifiers such as first name, last name, gender
  • Employee Information such as employee ID, job title,
  • Contact Information such as phone number, email address, emergency contact details
  • Incident details

Use of personal information


While you may report an Incident about you by being anonymous. However, if you wish to reveal your identity, we will process your personal information for variety of legitimate purposes, such as the following:

  • To verify your identity
  • To create and maintain records for internal evaluation, or other legitimate purpose
  • To report to our Ethics Committee or Management responsible for Ethics related matters

To report to our Ethics Committee or Management responsible for Ethics related matters

Sharing of personal information


We may share your personal information with our internal business functions for a variety of purposes and also with our group companies. Along with necessary functions or teams involved in investigation, the personal information shared by you will be received by the Global Ethics Office and other relevant stakeholders. We may disclose personal information if required to do so by law or in the good faith belief that such disclosure will help protect the safety, property or rights of Flipkart, our customers, Employees or other persons such as addressing crimes committed on Flipkart property or to respond to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal process. Employee Information may be disclosed to a law enforcement agency without your consent under prescribed circumstances. You understand that your information may be transferred to countries outside of your country of residence. In case you want to understand more on raising any ethics related request, please contact your regional HR team or write to ethics@flipkart.com.
